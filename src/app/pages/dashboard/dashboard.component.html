About

Experience Highlights

A concise view of key experience and impact from my work in quality engineering and frontend automation.

QA Process & Automation — Efani

Joined as the company's first QA hire and built the entire QA process from the ground up, including test case design, regression suites, and defect tracking workflows. Automated website regression testing using Playwright and built the CI/CD pipeline for the test repository, reducing manual regression time. Also tested Efani's mobile application, BSP (Black Seal Protection), covering functional and regression testing across releases. Alongside QA, I worked as a Jr. Frontend Developer on Efani's website, which is built in Angular, contributing to frontend development and gaining hands-on experience with the codebase I was also testing.

ECOWAS Resource Tracking Platform — K2X Technologies

Tested a resource-tracking platform for ECOWAS member countries that monitored natural resources such as oil across their full lifecycle, including refined output, industrial allocation, and cross-country shipping, visualized through an interactive Sankey diagram showing resource flow at each stage. Because the platform was heavily data-driven, testing involved extensive database validation to ensure resource data stayed accurate and consistent across every stage of tracking for each member country. Performed manual functional, integration, and regression testing within an Agile Scrum team.

Jobvine — Product Box

Tested Jobvine, a job and freelance marketplace mobile app connecting job seekers and recruiters, similar in concept to LinkedIn. Performed manual functional and regression testing across core user flows, including profile creation, job listings, and messaging between job seekers and recruiters.

Uqudo KYC App — Product Box

Tested the Uqudo KYC app, a digital identity verification platform enabling automated identity checks through ID document scanning and facial recognition. Performed manual functional and regression testing on document capture, facial verification steps, and edge cases across different devices.
